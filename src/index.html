Get To Know Us

Fhenix Africa is a dynamic tech startup based in Akwa Ibom, Nigeria, that focuses on providing innovative digital training.

The company was founded in 2020 by a group of ambitious entrepreneurs with a shared vision of leveraging technology to drive positive change and economic growth in Africa.

Our Tracks

Robotics

Welcome to the exciting world of robotics at the Aceplora TechCamp! From wiring circuits to coding the instructions that make your robots move, you will bring your imagination to life and create robots capable of performing amazing tasks. Using cutting-edge hardware and software, you’ll learn how to program sensors, motors, and other components to create robots that can navigate mazes, run errands, automate household appliances, perform complex tasks, and even battle other robots in thrilling competitions. Join us and discover the endless possibilities of robotics!
